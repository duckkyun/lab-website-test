Principal Investigator

Professor, Department of Biomedical Sciences, Yonsei University College of Medicine

Professor Ji-Hwan Ryu studies how gut and skin commensal microbiota regulate immune homeostasis, tissue development, and inflammatory disease. His group develops gnotobiotic animal models and microbiome-based experimental systems to uncover the mechanisms linking host tissues and resident microbes.

He received his Ph.D. in Immunology from Yonsei University after M.S. and B.S. training at Korea University. He has served as professor at Yonsei University College of Medicine since 2020, following appointments as associate professor, assistant professor, research professor, staff scientist, and postdoctoral fellow.
