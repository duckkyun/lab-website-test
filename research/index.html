Research Themes

  • Gut microbiome and immune homeostasis We examine how commensal microbes help establish balanced host immune responses.
  • Skin microbiota and inflammatory disease We study how skin dysbiosis and early-life microbial priming contribute to allergic inflammation.
  • Tissue-resident cell regulation by microbiota We aim to define resident cell populations whose development is controlled by commensal microbes.

Methods / Platforms

Key approaches include germ-free and gnotobiotic animal models, defined commensal colonization, dysbiosis models, and microbiome-based functional analysis.
